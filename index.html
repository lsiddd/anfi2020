HS2020: 11072 Advanced Networking and Future Internet

Instruction for the ANFI (HS2020: 11072) test exam, to be held on November 30, 2020, at 10:15am.

Requirements for the test:

  1. All students taking the test must write a declaration recognizing legal responsability for any attempted frauds in their tests, such as the consultation of unauthorized material, or consulting other participants and 3rd parties. The declaration can be found here.
  2. All participants taking the test must set up at least one camera device (such as laptop/smartphone camera) that shows the space in which they are taking the test, with a clear view of the papers they are writing in and their desk.

Instructions for the test exam

  1. On the day of the test exam, students must join the Zoom call through which lectures are discussed (link on the course page on ILIAS), prefferably with their students.unibe.ch account.
  2. The questions for the exam will be shown in slides through Zoom in three rounds of 15 minutes, with 5 minutes breaks between rounds.
  3. Students must position their computers (or the device used to join the class) far enough from themselves for them not to be able to interact with their computers during the class.
  4. All answers must be handwritten, prefferably in the corresponding question rounds on Zoom.
  5. After each round, students must photograph their response sheets and merge the images in PDF format, then send to the corresponding E-Mail address FROM THEIR @STUDENTS.UNIBE.CH EMAIL ACCOUNT, other accounts that do not correspond to the one registered on ILIAS will not be considered, to make sure of the sender's identity.
  6. Please write all your responses in clear an intelligible handwriting, unitelligible answers will not be considered.
